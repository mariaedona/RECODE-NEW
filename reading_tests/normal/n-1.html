How to create Personal Blog

Dikha Danar Wijanarko

I. Goal is to Create Personal Blog.
II. Things that you need to Create Personal Blog.
A. Materials
- Personal Computer/Laptop
- Internet Connection
- Modem
- Blogging Platform

III. Steps on how to create Personal Blog.
1. Prepare a personal computer/laptop and turn it on.

2. Install the modem into the computer.

3. Connect to internet by using the modem, wait until the internet is connected.

4. Open browser software in the computer.

5. Choose a free blogging platform, you can use any platform of blogging that you want. There are two most popular free blogging platforms (Blogger.com and Wordpress.com).

6. Register for an account and create your blog, you must fill every form with your real identity like name, address, email etc. Make sure that you fill every form correctly.

7. Select your domain, theme and others options, during the registration process for your new blog account, you'll need to choose a domain name and blog theme.

8. Start blogging, once you've completed steps 5-7, you're ready to join the blogosphere and start publishing content on your blog.
